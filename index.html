Worship at SRF

Public worship is a holy assembly called into existence by the Word of God.

Our response to God’s Word results in a dialogue that is both solemn and joyous.

When you worship at Somerset Reformed Fellowship, you will hear the thunder of God's Law, and confess your sins in comfort of the Gospel.

You will witness God's great work of redemption, and behold Christ's body broken for sinners.

Finally, you will be commissioned for a life of gratitude and service.
